HOW TO PLAY

Rules :

The game includes 2 players on a single screen.
Each player has a temporary score (CURRENT) and an overall score (GLOBAL).
At each turn, the player has his CURENT initialized to 0 and can roll a die as many times as he wishes. The result of a throw is added to the CURENT.
During his turn, the player can decide at any time to:
- Click on the “Hold” option, which sends the points of the CURENT to the GLOBAL.
It will then be theother player's turn.
- Roll the dice. If he rolls a 1, his CURENT score is lost and his turn ends.
The first player to reach 100 points on global wins the game.
